    About Voltiac Network

    Voltiac Network was first created on the 20th of January 2021. The current owner and co-owner merged their two server's Stonks Server (Hellohi3654) and RealmCrafters (Jacob_) to make Voltiac Network!

    Since then the server has grown into what it is now. We are constantly looking for new editions to add to our server and are always looking for inspiration.
